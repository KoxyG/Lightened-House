Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a personal styling appointment?

+

You can book a personal styling appointment by filling out the contact form above and selecting "Personal Styling Session" as your inquiry type, or by calling us directly at +234 (81) 0000-0000.

What is your return policy?

+

We offer a 30-day return policy for all unworn items with original tags attached. Items must be in their original condition. Custom or personalized items are not eligible for returns.

Do you offer alterations?

+

Yes! We offer complimentary alterations for all purchases over $300. For purchases under $300, alterations are available at a nominal fee. Please book an appointment for fittings.

Can I order custom pieces?

+

Absolutely! We specialize in custom designs for special occasions. Please schedule a consultation with our design team to discuss your vision and requirements.
